about us


Sirco Electric, was established in May of 2013 to service Victoria BC, as well as, Fly In Fly out energy operations throughout Canada. Our staff are knowledgeable, dedicated professionals who take pride in providing outstanding, personalized service to our customers. Call us today and become one of our many satisfied customers.

Company History

Chad Sirna started his electrical career as an apprentice working residential and commercial in Victoria BC. He later went on to explore opportunities in the oil and gas sector throughout northern BC and Alberta. Starting as a journeyman electrician, he shortly moved into quality control, Electrical Startup & Commissioning technician, and finally Electrical & Instrumentation Field engineering.

Incorporated in May of 2013, Sirco Electric continues to service industrial, commercial and residential services alike.

From replacing a receptacle, to commissioning a billion dollar energy project, no job is too small or too big for us to handle.
